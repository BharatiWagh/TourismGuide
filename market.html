Appa Balwant Chowk

Appa Balwant Chowk, conveniently called ABC, is for all book lovers. With wide price ranges, the stationary shops offer all you need to satisfy your stationery obsession. Hong-kong-lane

This super-popular street on Deccan Road is the place for shopping in Pune. The accessories like bags, books and mobile stores are what dominates this lane. Shops here are overloaded with mobile phone cases and decked with sunglasses, caps and wallets. Location:Hong Kong Lane, Deccan Gymkhana, Pune Timings: 10:00 AM - 7:30 PMKnow More....

Tulsibag

Tulsi Bag is in the heart of Pune and a haven for street shopping. Super famous for ready-made garments, you will find rows of shops available for your customised fittings here. This is one of the oldest markets for shopping in Pune equivalent to Delhi's Chandni Chowk.Location: 93, Laxmi Rd, Tulshibaug, Budhwar Peth Timings: 09:00 AM - 11:00 PMKnow More....

Fashion Street

One of the most popular places for shopping in Pune, Fashion Street will give you a wardrobe makeover at the budget price. The clothing here is so cheap! Similar to the one in Mumbai, these crowded narrow lanes offer anything and everything under the fashion canopy. Location: Bhavani Peth, Shobhapur, Kasba Peth, Pune Timings: 09:00 AM - 9:00 PMKnow More....

Empress Botanical Garden

The Empress Botanical Garden is named after Queen Victoria back when she held the sceptre. Once a favourite among the British officials and thus also named Soldier's Garden, the place is now a sprawling greenery of 39 acres with hundreds of rare species in trees and flowers. Location:Kavade Mala, Ghorpadi, Pune.Timing:All days, 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM. Know More....

Dr. Salim Ali Biodiversity Park

This tourist attraction is created as a tribute to Dr Salim Ali who is known as the " Birdman of India" and is an excellent place for bird watchers to witness various exotic birds and around 25 different species of butterflies.
